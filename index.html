Cloud Computing

Cloud computing refers to the delivery of computing services over the internet. It provides access to a shared pool of resources, including networks, servers, storage, applications, and services, that can be rapidly provisioned and released with minimal management effort.

Benefits of Cloud Computing:

  • Scalability: Scale resources up or down based on demand.
  • Cost Savings: Pay only for what you use, avoiding upfront infrastructure costs.
  • Flexibility: Access resources and data from anywhere, using any device.
  • Reliability: High availability and data redundancy.
  • Security: Robust security measures to protect data.

Cloud computing has revolutionized the IT industry, enabling organizations to focus on their core business objectives while leveraging the power and scalability of the cloud.
